Observation

My Assumptions

  • Simple trail that are not dangerous
  • Individual, quiet activity
  • Basic gear would suffice
  • Expected to see more tourists
  • Mostly young hikers

Reality Check

  • Steep, challenging rocky paths
  • Relatively quiet
  • Suffice for easy route, best to have equipment for harder
  • There were way more locals as compared to tourists
  • All ages, especially active seniors. There are even children hiking. People bring their pet to hike too!!

Reflection

"Gaming is not just entertainment in Korea - it's a legitimate culture and community"
- My PC Bang Experience

Cultural Understanding

PC Bangs revealed how deeply integrated gaming is in Korean society. It's not just entertainment, but a social space, workplace, and cultural hub all in one.

Technology Appreciation

The accessibility of high-end gaming technology democratizes competitive gaming. Anyone can access premium setups without huge personal investment.

Social Dynamics

The communal gaming experience creates unique social bonds. Playing together physically adds a dimension that online gaming alone cannot replicate.

Business Model Insights

The PC Bang model shows how shared resources can make expensive technology accessible while building community around common interests.
